Pennsylvania Small towns

WDD 130-[section]

Pennsylvania has rich history and a lot of it is in it's smaller towns. Gettysburg is a small town in PA with significant history that impacted the entire country. The battle of Gettysburg had the largest casualties in the Civil war. President Lincoln gave his historic Gettysburg address here. If your'e a history buff, this is the place to be. You may also want to check this town out during Halloween. Some of the best ghost tours are here.
